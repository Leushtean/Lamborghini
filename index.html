Portimao Track

The Autodromo Internacional do Algarve is located in the eponymous region in the south of Portugal, near the city of Portimão. Opened in October 2008, the motor racing complex is one of the most modern in Europe, spread over an area of around 300 hectares among the hills surrounding the Portuguese city, and the length of the track itself is 4684 meters.
